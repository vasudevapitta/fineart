Hey, I am Vasudeva.

I am a software developer by profession. Fine art is my passion. I grew up drawing and painting. Later I wanted to improve my art skills and I went to art school and I got an associate's degree in Fine Art from Parkland College, Illinois. I am very grateful to my professors Steven Hudson and Joan Stolz who taught me everything I know about art today. Charcoal and Oils are my favorite medium. I experiment with various subjects. Rembrandt, Rene magritte, Dali are some of my favorite artists. I love art and I would like to teach art to those who are passionate to learn. Be sure to check out some of my artworks below and feel free to say hi to me :)

Classes offered

  • Duration: 10 classes (90mins ea)

  • Fee: $20 per class

  • Topics:

  • Visualization
  • Elements of art
  • Line and contour
  • Shape and form
  • Composition
  • Space and depth
  • Shading and rendering
  • Light and shadow
  • Proportion and linear perspective
  • Projects and critique

  • Drawing II

  • Duration: 10 classes (90mins ea)

  • Fee: $20 per class

  • Topics:

  • In-depth investigation of various drawing media and papers
  • Design issues, expression
  • Trompe l'oeil
  • Envisioned and observational drawing
  • Head anatomy and portrait
  • Gesture and line movement
  • Advanced composition and atmospheric perspective
  • Realistic shading and rendering
  • Advanced perspective
  • Introduction to color theory
  • Projects and critique

  • Painting I

  • Duration: 10 classes (90mins ea)

  • Fee: $20 per class

  • Topics:

  • Introduction to oils, techniques and principles of oil painting
  • Techniques to transfer a drawing onto canvas
  • Preparation of painting surfaces and imprimatura
  • Wet on wet, thick and thin principles and technique
  • Development of color explanation of pictorial space
  • Color theory, color mixture and color psychology
  • Light and shadow
  • Landscapes
  • Still lifes and portrait
  • Projects and critique
